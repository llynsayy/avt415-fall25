The Psychology behind shy people

There is a difference between being shy and being an introvert. Being an introvert and being shy are not the same. Introverts want to be alone they choose to be alone while shy people want to engage in social connections but social anxiety kicks in.

Bedtime Procrastination

Also known as Revenge Bedtime Procrastination (RBP) is the act of going to sleep late to gain personal time for leisure or hobbies. This can lead to negative consequences like fatigue and can cause issues relating to sleep habits.

The relationship between art and music

Although music and art are both different when it comes to expression, there is symbiotic relationship between the two when it comes down to creativity.
